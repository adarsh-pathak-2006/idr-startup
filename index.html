ABOUT IDR

Bridging Academic Insight and Industry Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to developing exceptional digital, cyber, and technology risk professionals for regulated, high-consequence environments.

Through our strategic partnership with leading UK universities, we combine cutting-edge academic research with hands-on industry projects, creating a unique ecosystem where theory meets practice and talent meets opportunity.

WHAT WE DO

Service Pillars

A comprehensive ecosystem designed to train, deploy, and innovate in digital risk management.

Academy

Intensive training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals seeking to specialize in digital and cyber risk domains.

  • 12-week intensive bootcamps
  • Professional certification tracks
  • Executive education programs
  • Continuous learning modules

Innovation and Incubation

Developing intellectual property, next-generation risk models, and AI governance frameworks that shape the future of digital risk.

  • AI governance frameworks
  • Risk model development
  • IP creation and licensing
  • Research publications

Advisory Services

Expert guidance helping organizations implement and optimize industry-standard frameworks including NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

  • NIST CSF implementation
  • ISO 27001 certification support
  • NIS2 compliance programs
  • Regulatory gap analysis
